HUBS

START HUBS provide a physical space for early stage entrepreneurs and investors to connect, explore, and create new ventures.

Each HUB in the network is unique, but all HUBS provide one or more of the following facilities:

• Casual Networking Space: For individuals or small groups who need a casual place to meet and work or discuss about start-ups.

• Quiet Co-Working Space: For individuals who do not have a dedicated office and need a quiet environment with wifi to work.

• Collaborative Meeting Space: For groups or teams of 5-10 who need a private meeting room with whiteboard and projector. Individuals can also book these rooms for conference calls.

• Open Event Space: For individuals or groups who organize start-up related events and workshops for groups of 10-30 people.
