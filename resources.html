Resources

The START Network provides essential resources for early-stage tech start-ups. We group resources into three types:

• Knowledge: Essential learning from experienced entrepreneurs.

• Networks: Connections to potential core team members, employees, mentors, advisors, and investors based on common interests, job functions, or domain expertise.

• Financing: Access to channels for raising early-stage or growth capital, including debt, equity, and convertible debt.
