Design Principles for Start-ups

Want to Build Products that are “Insanely Great”? Come and Learn the Secret to Apple’s Success

At the core of Apple products like the iPhone is great design. Steve Jobs learned good design principles from his father and drew inspiration from companies like Porsche, Mercedes, and Braun. Jobs was passionate about building products that were “insanely great” in both form and function.

In this three hour workshop, you’ll learn the core principles of great design and have a chance to practice them in real-time with expert coaching from experienced designers.

Topics include:

• User Centered Design
• Visual Design Principles
• User eXperience (UX) Design for Web/Mobile Apps
• User Interface (UI) Design for Web/Mobile
• Examples of Good and Bad Design
• Where to find Great Design Inspiration

Who should attend?

• First-time entrepreneurs for web/mobile start-ups
• Technical Founders who lack formal Design Training/Experience
• Non-Technical (Business) Founders who lack formal Design Training/Experience
• Product Managers/Owners responsible for New Product Development
