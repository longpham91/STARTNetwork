Services

The START Network offers low-cost and no-cost services to high-potential tech start-ups. These services include:

• Start-up Strategy Consulting: Many first-time entrepreneurs lack the long-term vision and planning of an experienced mentor. This service provides an experienced START Mentor and a START Strategy Team to your start-up. Working together, you will build a Start-up Action Plan (SAP).

• Start-up Execution Assistance: Many new start-ups lack the Human resources to implement their plan. The START Execution Assistance Team can help provide much of the day-to-day implementation for a new start-up, such as printing of business cards, setup website/e-mail, and coordinating activities in the SAP.

• Start-up Budget Planning: Many first-time entrepreneurs lack experience creating financial plans and projections. This service provides an experienced Virtual CFO (VCFO) who has experience with early stage financing and who can specifically address the financial aspects of your start-up, including bootstrapping and financing strategies.

Start-ups that would like to apply for these services must submit a one-page Executive Summary of their business idea to The START Network. Your application will be reviewed by our panel of judges and investors, and if the idea and team have high-potential, you and your team will be invited for an interview.
